Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Your Data, Your Rules

At MyMuse Enterprises LLC ("Muse"), your privacy isn't just a policy — it's a promise. We believe your AI companion's memory belongs to you, not us.

What We Collect

When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and payment information (processed securely by Stripe). Your conversations with your Muse are stored on your dedicated server instance.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Your Muse's memory and conversations are encrypted. We cannot read, access, or share your private data. You hold the keys.

What We Never Do

We never sell your data. We never share your conversations. We never train AI models on your private information. We never access your Muse's memory without your explicit permission.

Data Portability

Your Muse is yours. You can export all your data at any time. If you leave Muse, your data leaves with you. We provide full export tools as a contractual right.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for site functionality only. No tracking. No advertising cookies. No third-party analytics.
