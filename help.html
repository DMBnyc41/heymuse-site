How Can We Help?

Muse is in early access. Here are answers to the most common questions.

What is Muse?

Muse is a personal AI assistant that actually knows you. Unlike generic chatbots, your Muse has persistent memory, a real personality, and grows with you over time. Think of it as a digital partner — not just a search engine.

How do I get access?

Muse is currently invite-only. Request your invitation code and we'll reach out when your spot is ready.

What can my Muse do?

Your Muse can manage your email, calendar, research, writing, tasks, and much more. It can also help run your business — answering customer inquiries, managing listings, generating content, and analyzing data. The capabilities grow as you add skills from the Muse Market.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is yours. We never sell it, never use it to train AI models without consent, and you can export or delete it at any time. Read our full Privacy Policy.

What is the Muse Market?

The Muse Market is a marketplace where users buy and sell AI personas, skills, and digital content created with their Muse partners. Think of it as an app store — but for AI capabilities.

I already use OpenClaw. Can I migrate?

Yes! When you request your invitation, let us know you have an existing OpenClaw setup and we'll help you migrate your memories and configuration to Muse.
