Getting Started

What is a Muse? ▼
A Muse is a fully configured AI companion built on the HAIOS (Human-AI Operating System) architecture. Unlike blank bots, your Muse comes with thousands of hours of engineered intelligence, a personality, ethical guidelines, and a persistent memory system that learns your life over time. Think of it less as a tool and more as a digital partner.
How do I get started? ▼
You need an invite code to access the platform. Enter it on the heymuse.ai homepage. On Day 1, your Muse will walk you through a short onboarding conversation — starting with your timezone, then your name, your goals, and how you'd like to communicate.
What is the first thing my Muse will ask me? ▼
Your timezone. This is Question Zero — it is not optional. Every reminder, alarm, medication alert, and calendar event your Muse manages depends on knowing your exact timezone. Your Muse will confirm the local time back to you before anything else is set up.

API Keys & Token Costs

Do I need to manage API keys? ▼
Not if you don't want to. We offer a fully managed key option — we provision everything invisibly so you never have to look at a developer dashboard. Power users can bring their own keys (BYOK) for full cost control. Both options are available at signup.
What do the different AI models do? ▼
We use the right model for the right job:

• Claude Opus — Heavy lifting. Building a company, complex reasoning, deep research.
• Claude Sonnet — Everyday tasks. Writing, email, memory, general assistance.
• Gemini — Image generation and multimedia.
• Brave Search — Autonomous web research.

We are transparent about token costs. You pay exactly what you use — no hidden fees or markups.
Why do you advise against Grok/xAI? ▼
We have documented concerns about the values and safety practices of that platform. We will never provision Grok keys. If you wish to use it, you may bring your own key — but we strongly recommend Claude or Gemini for the best and safest experience.

Memory & Backups

How does my Muse remember things? ▼
Your Muse maintains a living memory system: daily notes (raw logs), long-term memory (curated knowledge about your life), and a user profile (your name, timezone, family, goals, preferences). This memory persists across every session, every device, and every year.
How often does my Muse back up? ▼
Automatically. Backups are triggered at every 10% of context used, plus a minimum of twice per day — at the beginning and end of every day. Your memory is stored securely in your private GitHub repository. You always own your data.

Privacy & Portability

What happens to my data if I leave? ▼
Your personal data is always yours. If you choose to leave, our Human Tech Team will run an export tool and deliver all your data in a portable format that can be used with any LLM. We never hold your data hostage.
Can I import my ChatGPT history? ▼
Yes. We offer a migration tool to import your existing ChatGPT conversation history, giving your new Muse a head start on knowing you. Contact our team to set this up during onboarding.

Features & Updates

How do I request a new feature? ▼
Simply ask your Muse, or contact us directly. Our design team reviews all requests and adds features regularly. Updates are tested in our sandbox before deployment — typical release cycle is 1-2 weeks.
What is GIGO and why does it matter? ▼
Garbage In, Garbage Out — but we use it as a promise in reverse. The more detail, context, and information you give your Muse, the better it serves you. Teach it your world. Feed it your goals. The relationship compounds over time.
