Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

1. Subscription Cancellations

You may cancel your recurring Muse subscription at any time. Your cancellation will take effect at the end of the current paid term. You can cancel by contacting us at support@heymuse.ai.

2. Subscription Refunds

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for your first subscription charge. If you are unsatisfied within the first 7 days, please contact us for a full refund. Beyond 7 days, we do not offer prorated refunds for canceled subscriptions.

3. IT Consulting & Installation Services

For physical installations (e.g., security cameras, networking) and IT consulting, all sales are final once services have been rendered. Labor and specialized parts are non-refundable. If there is a defect with installed hardware, it will be handled according to the manufacturer's warranty.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund on an eligible purchase, please email support@heymuse.ai with your transaction details.
